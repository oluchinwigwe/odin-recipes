Indomie

Ingredients

  • Pack of indomie
  • water

Steps

  1. Boil some water in a pot on medium heat.
  2. Add the noodles from the indomie pack and cook until softened, about 2-3 minutes.
  3. Add the seasoning included in the pack and cook for about 1 minute.
  4. Serve with your protein of choice: chicken, shrimp, or beef.
