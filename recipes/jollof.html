Jollof rice

This is a delicious plate of jollof rice specially prepared with the freshest of tomatoes and peppers, creating the best taste in the world.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Canola oil
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Pepper
  • Bay leaf
  • Thyme
  • Curry
  • Seasoning cubes
  • Salt
  • Meat broth
  • Bell Pepper
  • Water

Steps

  1. Wash your rice thoroughly and set it aside.
  2. Heat the oil on low heat and add your diced onions, fry until transluscent. Then add the curry, bay leaf and thyme and fry for 30 seconds.
  3. Add the blended tomatoes and bell pepper and fry until the water is cooked down.
  4. add the meat broth and check the taste. Add salt and other spices until desired taste. Then add the washed rice.
  5. Add enough water to cover the rice and cook on low heat until the rice cooked.
  6. Let the rice cool and then enjoy!
