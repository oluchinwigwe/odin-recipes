Scrambled Eggs


scrambled eggs

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Cooking oil
  • Salt
  • Peppers
  • Onions
  • Fresh Tomatoes
  • Seasoning cubes

Steps

  1. Heat cooking oil in frying pan on low heat, add onions and fry until transluscent.
  2. Add the fresh tomatoes and peppers and fry.
  3. Once tomatoes are well fried, add the seasoning cubes and salt and addjust until you achieve the desired taste.
  4. Serve with bread, boiled yam or white rice as desired.
